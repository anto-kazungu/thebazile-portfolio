About Me

Living a Life that Loves the Earth and Lifts Others.

I’m a curious mind shaped by Economics and Psychology at Kenyatta University, where I learned how systems work — and more importantly, how people think and thrive within them. My journey began in the green heart of Ngong Forest, where I discovered the power of environmental action. That passion soon branched out into tech and community through programs like CSTEMX and CTK Youth, where I’ve worked to bridge the gap between creativity, technology, and opportunity. I thrive at the intersection of environmental sustainability, tech innovation, and creative expression — the tools I use to empower young people and build stronger, more conscious communities. For me, it’s not just about projects — it’s about purpose.
